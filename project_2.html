    • Journey Into Additive Manufacturing

    Two years ago, my exploration of additive manufacturing using FDM 3D printers commenced through my capstone project. Our team was tasked with developing a vibration-based sample mixer for Response Biomedical. Recognizing the necessity for rapid prototyping, especially for testing our models, I, being responsible for modeling, promptly adapted my designs to suit printing on the campus printers. Yet, it became evident that the standard 3D printers accessible to us lacked the capability we required. Consequently, I invested in my own 3D printer, marking the inception of my additive manufacturing journey.
